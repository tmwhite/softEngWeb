Team Red Wings Software Engineering Project

Our software lets you give a presentation from anywhere and easily. We focus on helping teachers in a classroom environment. Instead of the hassle of getting a presentation onto a computer, upload it and access it from anywhere. Instead of being forced to stand by your podium to give your presentation, walk around the classroom and use a phone or tablet as a remote control. Instead of just pointing to parts of the presentation, write on it. All of this while allowing anyone to connect to your presentation and watch it along with you in realtime.
